ðŸ‡®ðŸ‡¹ Spaghetti Carbonara Recipe

Country: Italy

Historical information:

Carbonara originated in Rome during WWII, when American soldiers introduced bacon and powdered eggs, inspiring local cooks to adapt it with Italian ingredients.

Difficulty: 3/5

Recipe:

1. Cook 400g spaghetti in salted boiling water until al dente.

2. Fry 150g pancetta until crispy.

3. In a bowl, whisk 3 large eggs with 100g grated Pecorino Romano and black pepper.

4. Drain pasta and return to the pot off heat.

5. Quickly toss pasta with pancetta and egg mixture until creamy.

6. Serve immediately with extra Pecorino and black pepper.

ðŸ‡¯ðŸ‡µ Sushi (Nigiri) Recipe

Country: Japan

Historical information:

Sushi evolved from fermented rice preservation in Southeast Asia, becoming refined in Edo (Tokyo) during the 19th century as a fast food.

Difficulty: 4/5

Recipe:

1. Rinse 300g sushi rice until water runs clear, cook, and let cool.

2. Mix rice with 3 tbsp rice vinegar, 1 tbsp sugar, and 1 tsp salt.

3. Slice 200g fresh fish into thin pieces.

4. Wet hands, form small rice oblongs.

5. Place a slice of fish over each rice piece, dab with wasabi if desired.

6. Serve with soy sauce, pickled ginger, and wasabi.

ðŸ‡¯ðŸ‡µ Ramen Recipe

Country: Japan

Historical information:

Ramen came from Chinese noodle soups but developed into a Japanese comfort food in the early 20th century. Post-WWII, it became a symbol of affordable nourishment.

Difficulty: 3/5

Recipe:

1. Prepare 1.5 liters chicken or pork broth, simmer with garlic, ginger, and soy sauce for 30 minutes.

ðŸ‡²ðŸ‡½ Mole Poblano Recipe

Country: Mexico

Historical information:

Mole is tied to Puebla's colonial history, said to have been created by nuns who blended Old World and Indigenous ingredients.

Difficulty: 5/5

Recipe:

1. Toast 3 dried chiles (ancho, pasilla, mulato) and soak in hot water.

2. Blend chiles with 50g almonds, 30g raisins, 2 garlic cloves, 1 onion, and 30g dark chocolate.

3. Simmer mixture with 500ml chicken broth for 20 minutes.

4. Season with cinnamon, cloves, and salt.

5. Pour over cooked chicken and simmer for 15 minutes.

ðŸ‡®ðŸ‡³ Biryani Recipe

Country: India

Historical information:

Originating in Persia and refined by Mughal kitchens, biryani is now a festive dish with countless regional versions.

Difficulty: 5/5

Recipe:

1. Marinate 500g meat with yogurt, ginger, garlic, and spices for two hours.

ðŸ‡¹ðŸ‡­ Green Curry Recipe

Country: Thailand

Historical information:

Thai curries trace back centuries, blending local herbs with influences from Indian and Chinese spice routes.

Difficulty: 4/5

Recipe:

1. Blend 4 green chilies, garlic, lemongrass, and galangal into a paste.

2. Fry paste in 2 tbsp oil, add 400ml coconut milk.

3. Add 300g chicken and cook 10 minutes.

4. Add eggplant and simmer until tender.

5. Stir in fish sauce, sugar, and Thai basil. Serve hot.
